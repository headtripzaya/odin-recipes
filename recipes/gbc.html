Gooey Butter Cake

Description

The most banger gooey butter cake you've ever had

Ingredients

  • 1 (18.25 ounce) packaged yellow cake mix
  • 1/2 cup butter, melted
  • 4 large eggs, divided
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract, divided
  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
  • 4 cups of confectioners' sugar

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F(175 degrees C)
  2. Mix cake mix, melted butter, 1 teaspoon vanilla, and 2 eggs with a spoon. Pat into a 9x13-inch pan.
  3. Mix cream cheese, remaining 2 eggs, and remaining 1 teaspoon vanilla with an elextric mixer. Slowly beat in confectioners sugar. Pour over cake layer.
  4. Bake for 40 to 45 minutes. Cool
