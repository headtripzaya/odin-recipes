Homemade Brownies

Description

The most banger brownies you've ever had

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flower
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1//4 teaspoon baking powder

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F(175 degrees C). Grease and flour an 8-inch square pan.
  2. Spread batter into prepared pan. Bake in preheated oven for 25 to 30 minutes. Do not overcook.
